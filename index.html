Applying Flexibilities to Natural Health Products (NHPs)

Level 1:

  • Move Uses to Principal Display Panel
  • Remove hairlines from "Warnings" section.
  • Remove text immediately following "Non-Medicinal Ingredient" or "Warning" heading.
  • Reduce weight of rules to 1.0 pt.

Level 1 flexibilities not shown below:

  • Remove "Product Facts Continued" heading.
  • Continuous box frame with conditions.


Product Facts

Visit www.name-nom.ca.

Medicinal ingredient

Ginger extract 25%, equivalent to 100mg ginger dried root

Uses •To prevent symptoms of digestive upset •indigestion •lack of appetite

Warnings

Do not exceed 12 capsules a day. Do not exceed the recommended dosage.

Consult a healthcare practitioner if. •symptoms worsen

Keep out of reach of children.

Directions •Adults and children 12 years or older: take 2 capsules every 6 hours up to 4 times a day

Other information Store between 10-30°C.

Non-medicinal ingredients orange essential oil, acacia, citric acid, paraffin.

Questions? Call 1-XXX-XXX-XXXX

Level 2:

  • Use of condensed font

Level 2 flexibility not shown below:

  • Use of scaling (up to 90%)

Level 3:

  • Move contents of "Other Information", such as storage information, elsewhere on the outer label.
  • Move "Non-Medicinal Ingredients" elsewhere on the outer label. Subheading must be maintained in Product Facts Table (PFT) with note directing consumer to where the information is located (for example, "See bottom of carton").
  • Use of common or proper name. The full information must be available on a URL (use of additional package or insert or leaflet optional)
        >>URL that contains full PFT required
  • "Source of" or potency information can be moved to a URL (use of additional package insert or leaflet optional).
